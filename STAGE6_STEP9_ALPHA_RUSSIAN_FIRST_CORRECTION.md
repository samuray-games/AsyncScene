## 2. Language hierarchy

### I0_CRITICAL

Default: Russian-first and immediately understandable.

Examples:

- `го`
- `правила`
- `репорт`
- `отмена`
- `ещё`
- `в бой`

Do NOT default to:

- `start`
- `rules`
- `report`
- `cancel`
- `rematch`

unless a specific borrowed token is genuinely more natural and explicitly approved.

### I1_GUIDANCE

Mostly Russian syntax with compact code-like structure.

Good direction:

- `нужно 2💰`
- `репорт ✓`
- `чекаю`
- `→ {location}`
- `ещё нельзя`
- `КД`

Bad direction:

- `checking`
- `move > {location}`
- `event locked`
- `wait result`

when a natural Russian Alpha rendering exists.

### I2_EXPRESSIVE

Russian base may mix with authentic borrowed markers.

Good:

- `W`
- `L 💀`
- `репорт W`
- `бро cooked`
- `аура −1000`
- `NPC тейк`
- `mid`
- `W мув`

The sentence does not need to be pure Russian, but the profile must still read as Russian-speaking rather than English-localized.

### I3_FULL_FREEDOM

Heavy brainrot/code mixing is allowed when natural for the exact semantic context.

Examples:

- `бро чё 💀`
- `NPC момент`
- `L тейк`
- `аура −1000`
- `cooked`

Even here, English density is a stylistic option, not a mandatory default.
